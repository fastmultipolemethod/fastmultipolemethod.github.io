Welcome

This website is developed by a group of people working on integral equations (IEs) and fast algorithms such as the fast multipole methods (FMM) for large scale simulations. It provides a collection of tutorials developed by us or our colleagues on these topics, a wide selection of open-source implementations of the multipole methods on shared and distributed memory architectures, and links to many research papers, categorized by individual application fields.

We hope you find this website informative and useful and welcome you to join our effort.
